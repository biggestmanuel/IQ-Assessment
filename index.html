Before you begin, read these rules carefully.

📋
30 Questions
You will answer 30 questions drawn from six cognitive categories — Verbal Reasoning, Numerical Sequences, Pattern Recognition, Spatial Reasoning, Logical Deduction, and Odd One Out.
⏱
45 Seconds Per Question
Each question has a strict 45-second timer. The timer turns orange at 20 seconds and red at 10 seconds. If the timer runs out, the question is automatically skipped and you cannot return to it.
⏭
Next Button — Timer Pauses
Pressing Next pauses the clock at whatever time remains. When you return to that question, the timer resumes from exactly where it left off — not from 45 seconds.
🚫
Skip = Permanent
The Skip button permanently removes the question. You cannot return to a skipped question under any circumstances. Skipped questions are counted against your score. Use it wisely.
🚨
Do Not Leave This Tab
Switching tabs, minimising the browser, or navigating away will immediately terminate your test. Your session will be invalidated and you will have to start over. Stay on this page for the entire duration.
🧠
Scoring & IQ Range
Your final IQ score is calculated instantly at the end. You will receive an IQ range (e.g. 115–120), a percentile ranking, and a written assessment of your performance. Results can be shared to WhatsApp, Telegram, and more.
